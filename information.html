Blood Importance

DONORS GUIDELINES:-

Eligibilities:-

1) Any donor, who is healthy, fit and not suffering from any transmittable diseases can donate blood.

2) Donor must be 18 -60 years age and having a minimum weight of 50Kg can donate blood.

3) Donor’s Hemoglobin level is 12.5% minimum.

4) A donor can again donate blood after 3 months of your last donation of blood.

5) Pulse rate must be between 50 to 100mm without any irregularities.

6) BP Diastolic 50 to 100 mm Hg and Systolic 100 to 180 mm Hg.

7) Body temperature should be normal and oral temperature should not exceed 37.5 degree Celsius.

Non-Eligibilities:-

1) Donors should not suffer from Cardiac arrest, hypertension, kidney alignments, epilepsy or diabetics.

2) Ladies with a bad miscarriage should avoid donating blood for the next 6 months.

3) If donor already donated blood or have been treated for malaria within the last three months.

4) If donor undergone any immunization within the past one month.

5) If donor consumed alcohol within the last 24 hours.

6) If you are HIV+.

7) If donor had a dental work for next 24 hours and wait for one month if donor had a major dental procedure.

Preparation:-

i) Prepare yourself by having enough fruit juice and water in the night and morning before you donate blood.

ii) Avoid donating blood in empty stomach. Eat three hours before you donate blood. Avoid fatty foods. Eat food which is rich in iron such as whole grains, eggs, and beef, and spinach, leafy vegetables, orange and citrus.

iii) Don’t consume Alcohol or caffeine beverages before donating blood.

iv) Avoid donating blood for 6 months if you had any major surgery.

How to Relieve:-

While you are being needled to take blood, relax to the maximum, have a deep breath. There will be little pain or no pain. Try to distract yourself from the process by having a chewing gum. Listen to some music or watch some entertaining TV program.

HOW TO DONATE BLOOD?

You may please visit a Clinical Laboratory center and identify your blood group. The clinical test will cost you just ` 50-60. The result shall be provided within 2 hours time. In case you wish to donate blood now, please visit your nearest Blood Collection Center. Alternatively you may use the Registration Form given here in the website and submit for registration. Recipient, relatives or Hospital authorities will contact you directly.
